Attractions

For any questions, comments, or suggestions, feel free to get in touch with us via phone or email. We’re happy to help you and look forward to hearing from you!

Phnom Penh is a haven for all sorts of travelers and has many different attractions and sights to offer. Whether you’re an adventurer who wants to go on city hikes and walks, or if you’re looking to explore the city’s culture and nightlife — Phnom Penh is the right address for you. Discover below the many different things we offer you at the hotel and find out what to discover around us!

POOL AND LOUNGE DECK

What could be better than enjoying the cool water on a hot day? A cold drink on the lounge deck, that’s what! Located right next to our Sky Bar, the pool and lounge deck are sure to make you feel chill
