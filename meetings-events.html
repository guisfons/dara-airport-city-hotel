Meetings
& Events

Dara Airport City Hotel & Spa has large and fully equipped halls, boardrooms, as well as event and meeting spaces for team-building events and marketing launches. Booking a private meeting and event space means you will also get to try our renowned Dara Airport Catering service with great food and beverages. There’s also a Business Center which is available 24/7 and has multilingual staff to assist you with any questions.

LINDBERGH 1 & 2

Lindbergh 1: 240 sqm - up to 180 guests.
Lindbergh 2: 320 sqm - 200 to 250 guests.

If connected, both together can host up to 500 guests for banquets, film screenings, team building or other special events.
